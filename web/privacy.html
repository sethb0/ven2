Venator

We kill Anathema from the command line.

Venator is a command-line-based utility suite for creating, manipulating and rendering Exalted 2.5e character sheets.

Venator does not collect any data about users. This website does not use cookies or require a user account. If you do not use Venator's Google Docs integration feature, you will not be asked for any user account information or other personal information, and no data will be transmitted to any other computer.

If you choose to use the Google Docs integration feature, you will be referred to the Google website to enter your password; it will not be received or stored by Venator. Logging into your Google account is necessary so that Venator can upload your character sheets in Google Docs format. Venator does not access or download any files that already exist on your Google Drive. Venator stores temporary data (an "access token" and "refresh token") on your computer to enable you to upload files without logging in every time. No other data is transmitted to Google. Venator never sends any data to any other computer except as specifically described in this paragraph.
